ABOUT US

Western South Dakota's
Precision Ag Partner

Pyro Ag Services LLC is a family-owned custom application business based in western South Dakota, founded in the spring of 2024. The company specializes in precision application using both agricultural drones and a self-propelled sprayer, providing efficient and modern solutions for today's producers.

In addition to custom application services, Pyro Ag Services has expanded its offerings to include sales of Gteex agricultural drones, Lane trailers, application software, and Windfall drift control agent. By combining advanced technology with dependable service, Pyro Ag Services is committed to helping producers improve efficiency and maximize results in the field.
